Roses are perennial flowering plants known for their beautiful, fragrant blooms and thorny stems. They belong to the family Rosaceae, which also includes other popular plants like apples, cherries, and strawberries.

Roses pictures

A typical rose flower has multiple petals arranged in a whorl around a central core. The number of petals can vary significantly depending on the species and cultivar, ranging from a simple five-petaled flower to complex, multi-layered blooms with dozens of petals.
